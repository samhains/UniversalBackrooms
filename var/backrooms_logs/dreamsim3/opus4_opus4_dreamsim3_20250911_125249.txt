> start reversing

> swerve around people

Metal CRUNCHES as you clip something. Still accelerating backward. The brake pedal has disappeared entirely.

> get out

You grab the door handle and throw yourself from the moving vehicle. The pavement rises to meet you but you land soft, rolling through space that feels like thick air. Behind you, the car continues its backward rampage before vanishing into a wall that wasn't there before.

You're in a different car park now. Smaller. School buildings loom with too many windows.

> examine ferrari

A gleaming red Ferrari sits perfectly centered in a parking space. Its paint seems wet, liquid, reflecting a sky that isn't there. Three men stand around it in perfect triangular formation.

Their faces are covered in surgical green masks that extend too far, merging with their skin. Their boots are the same green, rubbery, reaching up to their knees. Green pointed hats sit at impossible angles on their heads.

They haven't noticed you yet. Or they're pretending not to.

>
