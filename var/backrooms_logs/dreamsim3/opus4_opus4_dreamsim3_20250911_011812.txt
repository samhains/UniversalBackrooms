"--driving through the mountains with windows down--"

"--the idea forming like condensation--"

The story spirals inward to this moment, this light, this binding.

> place creation in fire pit

"We did it," you say, and everyone nods because they were always part of the 'we,' even when you thought you were working alone.

"We found out how to make light."

"We found out we ARE light," someone corrects, and it might be you.
